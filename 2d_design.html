  • 20201007 Lyu Yitong Project4 Value Scale color-aid 2d design

    In this project, we will use acrylic paint, which includes white, dark blue and burnt brown. We found out as the best choice by making 30 grey mixtures. Through this project, we realized that the value / value scale is a series of steps, from black to white, with gray in the middle. The value in the scale is from light to dark or from dark to light. Each step in a series is a gap between the two sides: half dark, one in front, twice as black as the one behind. We need to paint the colors on the paper and cut them into small squares 1.5 inches high by 1 inch wide and stick them to the Bristol board.
