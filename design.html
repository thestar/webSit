Introduction of freshman design course

This course offers an introduction to Graphic Design, Industrial Design and Interior Architecture. Specific course content will include knowledge and skill that is important to each discipline, including conducting research, sketching, visual documentation & communication, graphic design basics and mock-ups or prototyping.

  • 202000917 Yi tong Lyu Architectural design

    The goal of this project is to learn to draw perspectives from the basics. We discussed parallel lines, horizontal lines, vanishing points, and one - and two-point perspectives. We need to discuss the use of a two-point perspective in a drawing to design a building. We can use the drawing paper to feel the different feelings brought by different painting paper. In this assignment, I used rough brown cardboard and pencil to draw two perspective coffee shops.

  • 20200910 Yi tong lyu two Perspective Drawing sketchbook

    The goal of this project is to learn to draw perspectives from the basics. We discussed parallel lines, horizontal lines, vanishing points, and one - and two-point perspectives. We need to discuss the use of two-point perspective in drawing to design some overlapping cuboids, and we use light gray markers to show the shadow of light on objects. We can use the drawing paper to feel the different feelings brought by different painting paper. In this assignment, I used white paper and light gray marker to draw the light and shadow when two rectangles overlapped.
